! Copies the characers from src into destination, including the null terminator.

The strcpy() function copies the string pointed to by src, including the terminating null byte (\textquotesingle{}\textbackslash{}0\textquotesingle{}), to the buffer pointed to by dest. The strings may not overlap, and the destination string dest must be large enough to receive the copy. Beware of buffer overruns!
